What's the Workshop about?

You’re an experienced English teacher working with adults. That’s why you’re here.
And that’s why this workshop is for you.

We would like to introduce you to the new teaching method that we have been developing within our interdisciplinary transnational team of educators, teachers, methodologists and all the professionals who care about the high quality of learning outcomes. The IC-English method is a set of tools that consists of training materials for adult learners, a practical e-handbook and e-learning lessons that are addressed to you to help you fully use all the capabilities of what we’ve created.

During the workshop we will ask you to share with us feedback on the method and all the tools associated with it. Your voice is what improves our materials and makes them better adjusted to the real needs of learners and teachers/trainers.

Main Features of the Method

  • 3 English language competence levels (A1, A2 and B1) broken up to 90 class scenarios
  • lesson plans supported by web-based multimedia
  • using Kolb's effective learning cycle
  • using Honey and Mumford’s 4 styles of learning as the starting point for an individual approach to adult learners

    Meet our Experts

    Monika Belina

    Monika is English studies graduate with teaching credentials, PhD studies, 2 PASE publications, TELC A1-B2 examiner. She’s got 17-year experience in general and business English teaching at A1-C2 levels in various age groups, as well as in substantive preparation of classes, diagnosing the level of participants, test preparation, conducting trainings for teachers in interactive board operation, the use of online resources for language teaching.

    Isabel graduated in Modern Languages and Literatures – English and German, and holds two Master’s degrees: in Anglo-American Studies (English Literature) from the University of Lisbon, and in Human and Social Sciences Research from the Pontifical University of Salamanca.
